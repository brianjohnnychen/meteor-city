Consumer hardware is usually designed on instinct and validated after the fact. We inverted that: validation comes first, and the hardware follows.

01 — Origin

Founded July 2021.

Meteor City was founded in Boston, Massachusetts by Brian Chen, a software engineer who spent years in health care technology watching what disciplined data could do — how well-organized signals turned uncertain decisions into engineering problems. The company was built to bring that same rigor somewhere it rarely exists: everyday consumer products.

The premise has held. With enough well-organized market data, launching a physical product stops being a gamble. Validation comes first; the hardware follows.

02 — How we operate

Three rules the company runs on.

R1

Measure before making

No product enters design until Skyros can describe the gap it fills quantitatively. Instinct proposes; data disposes.

R2

Own the whole chain

Analysis, design, manufacturing oversight, freight, compliance, and channel operations are all managed in-house. Accountability doesn't outsource well.

R3

Choose, don't chase

Retail is the public surface of the company; most of the work isn't. New partnerships tend to begin on our initiative rather than by application — the same discipline that makes the data trustworthy makes the company quiet.
