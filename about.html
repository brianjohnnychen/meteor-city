01 — Company

Founded July 2021.

Meteor City LLC was founded in Boston, Massachusetts by Brian Chen, a software engineer who came out of health-care technology. The company designs and sells computer and workspace hardware.

Product decisions run through Skyros, an internal data platform that watches consumer marketplaces. Products ship under the Elestoria brand — at retail through Amazon, Walmart, and Target, and at wholesale to organizations. Alongside its own line, the company does market-analysis and launch consulting for manufacturers.
